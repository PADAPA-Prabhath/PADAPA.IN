Mission

  1. To introduce arts as a mainstream subject in schools, so that every child inculcates values organically.
  2. To expose every child to majority arts forms in the World, so that, each child can develop a new perspective of embracing beauty irrespective of history and background.
  3. To create job opportunities to established as well as amateur artists.
  4. To help deliver the right content to the students by developing researched syllabus and training techniques.
